Family Businesses:

Maine Custom Woodlands

[Maine Custom Woodlands]

My father, Thomas Cushman, founded Maine Custom Woodlands in 1993. He majored in Forest Engineering at the University of Maine. Maine Custom Woodlands is a timber harvesting and forest management business. The services provided by the company are: timber harvesting, excavation, commercial clearing and culverts. Along side Maine Custom Woodlands, he owns MCW Trucking. The trucking company is a sister company for the purpose of trucking the forest products for Maine Custom Woodlands. These products are hauled to various places such as ReEnergy, Hancock Lumber, Kennebec Lumber, various firewood businesses and many more. The shop and office are located in Durham, Maine.

William Allen Farm

[William Allen Farm]

My mother and step dad, Heidi and Al Curry, own a wedding venue that is the barn at our house in Pownal, Maine. There is a wedding almost every weekend through May all the way to the beginning of November. People who get married here are from all over. It is very interesting to see all of the different weddings each weekend. The barn is a blank slate so those who are getting married hire caterers, photographers, florists and many more businesses to come in and get the barn ready.
